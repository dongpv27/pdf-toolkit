## 7. ICON STRATEGY  (maximize CTR in search results)

- **Core symbol:** a clean document/page with a bold **"PDF"** label — instantly communicates the category in a crowded search list.
- **Add one motion cue** of the hero feature: a small photo/image glyph turning into a page (image→PDF), or a subtle merge arrow. This differentiates from generic "PDF reader" icons.
- **Color:** high-contrast, saturated accent (deep red or indigo) on a light/white background. Red reads as "PDF" by convention (Adobe association) and pops on the white Play results background.
- **Flat & simple:** no fine detail or long text — the icon renders at ~48 px in search. Big shape, big contrast, one focal element.
- **Consistency:** match the in-app primary color so the store→app transition feels coherent (boosts trust → conversion).
- **A/B test** 2 variants in Play Console (Store Listing Experiments): "PDF page only" vs "PDF page + image→arrow". Keep the winner.
